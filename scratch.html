What Is This?

This page is for people who actually watch the games. Who study the tape. Who know the difference between a screen-and-roll and a Spain pick-and-roll. Welcome.

The Ball Knowledge Tiers

Where do you fall? Be honest.

  1. Casual Fan
    • Knows LeBron, Steph, KD
    • Watches the Finals only
    • Gets scores from Twitter
  2. League Pass Watcher
    • Knows all 30 starting lineups
    • Watches 4+ games per week
    • Has a favorite player who isn't a superstar
  3. Advanced Stats Guy
    • Knows what RAPTOR and EPM mean
    • Can explain true shooting percentage
    • Watches film, not just highlights
  4. Elite Ball Knower
    • Knows bench players on bad teams
    • Can name G-League call-ups by team
    • Watches summer league voluntarily
    • Has opinions on two-way contracts
