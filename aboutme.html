About Me

Who I Am, What I Value, Where I'm Headed

Hi, I'm Ben Asay

I'm a Pre-Business student at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, with a focus on Information Systems. I'm passionate about understanding how technology and data can be used to make smarter business decisions.

When I'm not studying or working, you'll find me in the gym, reading, or deep-diving into NBA statistics and player history. I believe in working hard, staying curious, and always looking for ways to improve.
